Urgent & Primary Care

Your foundation for lifelong health - comprehensive care when you need it, without an appointment.

No Appointment Necessary for Urgent Care

Your whole family needs to visit a family physician regularly to maintain optimal health and receive routine physicals and preventative care. Recent research shows that adults who visit their primary care doctor regularly better manage chronic conditions, maintain lower healthcare costs, and are more satisfied with care.

Our physicians and experienced medical team are available to provide the services you need without an appointment. We offer flexible hours seven days a week and are even open on holidays!

Meet Our Physicians

Board-certified family medicine doctors dedicated to your health and wellbeing.

Dr. Efi Kessous, MD, MPH

Board-Certified in Family Medicine

Dr. Efi Kessous is board-certified in Family Medicine and currently practices Primary and urgent care medicine at Montgomery Medical Clinic. He is also an Assistant professor and a faculty member at The George Washington University School of Medicine & Health Sciences.

His clinical interests include Procedural Family medicine, injuries and lacerations, women's health, Occupational and travel medicine. He is currently a member of the American Academy of Family Physicians and the FAA aviation medical examiners.

Dr. Jonathan Lawless

Board-Certified in Family & Sports Medicine

Dr. Lawless is board-certified in family medicine and sports medicine. He completed his training at Northwell Plainview Hospital in Plainview, NY. He graduated from medical school at Lake Erie College of Osteopathic Medicine in 2012.

His skills include musculoskeletal care, diagnostic musculoskeletal ultrasound, ultrasound-guided musculoskeletal injections, osteopathic manipulative treatment, and trigger point injections.
