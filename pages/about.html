About Montgomery Medical Clinic

Your trusted, community-focused healthcare partner for over 50 years.

OUR MISSION

A Legacy of Compassionate Care

Our mission at Montgomery Medical Clinic is to provide comprehensive, high-quality, and affordable medical treatment for the whole family. We are committed to fostering a patient-provider relationship built on trust and mutual respect. Our goal is to be your partner in health, offering personalized care that considers all aspects of your well-being.

With a legacy spanning over five decades, we combine the wisdom of experience with the latest advancements in medical technology to deliver superior healthcare. We strive to be a pillar of health in the Gaithersburg community, ensuring every patient feels heard, cared for, and confident in their treatment.

Meet Our Expert Providers

Our team consists of board-certified physicians with extensive experience and a shared passion for patient-centered care. We are dedicated to building lasting relationships to better serve your healthcare needs.

Dr. Efi Kessous, MD, MPH

Board-Certified in Family Medicine

Dr. Efi Kessous is a board-certified Family Medicine physician and a faculty member at The George Washington University School of Medicine. His clinical interests include procedural family medicine, women's health, and occupational medicine. He is a designated FAA aviation medical examiner.

Dr. Eran Kessous, MD

Board-Certified in Family & Sports Medicine

Dr. Eran Kessous completed his sports medicine training at Harvard Medical School. He specializes in preventing, diagnosing, and treating injuries for athletes of all levels and was a team physician for the MLS team D.C. United and currently consults for the Kirov Academy of Ballet.

Dr. Jonathan Lawless, DO

Board-Certified in Family & Sports Medicine

Dr. Lawless is dual board-certified in family and sports medicine. His expertise includes diagnostic musculoskeletal ultrasound, ultrasound-guided injections, and osteopathic manipulative treatment. He has provided medical coverage for collegiate sports, marathons, and triathlons.
